Our Mission

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the hydration reminder work?

The bottle glows gently based on your hydration goals and time of day.

Can I wash it?

Yes, the bottle is water-resistant and the electronic base is detachable.

Does it work without the app?

Basic glow reminders work offline. Full tracking needs the app.
